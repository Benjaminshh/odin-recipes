Basic Chicken Lemon Marinade

Description

This is another basic lemon marinade that I found on a website

It seems really simple which is why I decided to list it

Ingredients

    • 1 cup olive oil
      1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
      1/2 cup water
      3 cloves garlic, peeled and smashed
      2 teaspoons honey
      2 teaspoons salt
      1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Make the marinade.Place the ingredients in a large ziplock bag
